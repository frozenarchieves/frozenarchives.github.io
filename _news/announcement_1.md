 üåä **SEALS to the Labrador Sea! üß≠**
---
Starting **June 3rd, 2025**, I'll be embarking on a month-long research expedition aboard R/V Roger Revelle for **SEALS 2025** (*Sediment Exchange Along Labrador Sea*) to investigate neodymium and iron fluxes in the Labrador and Greenland coasts ‚Äî a critical region for understanding deep ocean circulation and climate change.
We'll be collecting seawater and sediment samples across multiple stations, working with an amazing team to uncover what lies beneath the waves.
---

üß≠ Follow the journey and learn more at: [seals2025.com](https://seals2025.com)
