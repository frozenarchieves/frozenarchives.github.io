🌊 Expedition Alert: Heading to the Labrador Sea! 🧭

Starting June 3rd, 2025, I’ll be embarking on a month-long research expedition aboard R/V Roger Revelle for SEALS 2025 (Sediment Exchange Along Labrador Sea) to investigate neodymium and iron fluxes in the Northwest Atlantic — a critical region for understanding deep ocean circulation and climate change. We’ll be collecting seawater and sediment samples across multiple stations, working with an amazing team to uncover what lies beneath the waves. —

🧭 Follow the journey and learn more at: seals2025.com Read my intro blog @ SEALS2025_Intro

📅 Departure: June 3rd, 2025 📍 Route: Woods Hole → Labrador Sea → Reykjavik
🧪 Focus: Nd & Fe benthic fluxes, sediment-pore water exchange
⏱️ Duration: 33 days (7 transit + 26 science days).
